Why this page exists

Why we’re doing this

Over the last few tours, ticket resale and dynamic pricing have pushed many families out. Shows selling out in minutes. Seats relisted for hundreds or thousands of dollars. Parents trying to give their kids a first concert and realizing it’s no longer possible.

This page exists to restore clarity and dignity to that process.

I help fans understand what options may still exist when a show looks impossible, whether that means checking nearby cities, tracking limited releases, or being honest when nothing reasonable is available.

No pressure. No promises. Just clarity.

How fan support works

1. You reach out
You share the city you were hoping for and how many tickets you need.

2. I check what’s realistic
That may include nearby dates, small availability changes, or confirming that nothing affordable exists right now.

3. You decide
There is no obligation. If it makes sense, great. If it doesn’t, you walk away informed.

I do not resell tickets at inflated prices and I do not encourage overspending.

A quick clarification

  • This is not Ticketmaster
  • This is not resale or scalping
  • This does not guarantee tickets
  • This does not involve bidding or markups

This page is shared only with fans who publicly requested help or information.

What I can offer is transparency, advocacy for fans, and respect for your budget.

This page is for you if

  • You were priced out of a show
  • Tickets sold out before you had a chance
  • You were trying to buy for your kids or family
  • Resale prices made you step back
  • You just want to understand your options clearly

If that’s you, you’re in the right place.
